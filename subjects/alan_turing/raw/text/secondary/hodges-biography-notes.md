## Externalized soma (the billboard a camera would have seen)

1. Bitten fingernails; restless hands. (Chronic, adult life.)
2. Speech: a hesitation often described as a stammer — not fluency
   failure but selection: pausing mid-sentence while the thought
   completed; worsened under social pressure.
3. A sudden, high-pitched laugh colleagues found startling; arrived
   abruptly, often at structural absurdities no one else had noticed yet.
4. Long-distance running as discharge: serious club marathoner (best
   2:46, near-Olympic standard, 1947); reportedly explained he had a
   stressful job and running was how he got it out of his mind.
5. Indifference to dress and grooming to the point of legend; pyjama
   jacket under a sports coat; string or a tie as belt.

## Practical eccentricity / first-principles living

6. Bletchley: chained his mug to a radiator pipe (theft-proofing by
   direct mechanism); cycled in a gas mask against hay fever; counted
   pedal revolutions to time a slipping chain rather than repairing it
   — fixed the fault by mathematics instead of maintenance.
7. Buried silver bullion as a wartime hedge, then couldn't find it —
   trusted his own protocol over institutions, twice.

## Social pattern

8. Direct to the point of social friction; took questions literally;
   contemptuous of small talk, warm and patient with anyone who
   engaged the actual problem, notably children.
9. Candid about his homosexuality with friends in an era when candor
   was dangerous; at arrest, volunteered a full written statement —
   police described it as without shame. Refused to pretend wrongness
   he did not feel.
10. Uneasy and withdrawing at formal occasions; came alive in
    one-to-one technical conversation.

## Death

11. Died 8 June 1954, cyanide poisoning; inquest ruled suicide; his
    mother maintained accident (careless chemistry). The ambiguity is
    itself in-character data: he kept his interior weather private to
    the end. This archive records the fact and both readings, and
    builds nothing on either.
